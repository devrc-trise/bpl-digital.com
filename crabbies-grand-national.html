Goals

In January 2015 Jockey Club Catering requested a mobile ordering, payment and feedback solution for the Crabbie’s Grand National meeting commencing on 9th April 2015 until 11th April 2015

Streamline the ordering process for customers in two new stands and at four bars around the general admissions area

Additionally Aintree Racecourse wanted to present a forward thinking and service focused solution

Scope

The Grand National Fast Track app was available to all visitors across the three days of the meeting

Core functionality was to order and pay ahead for food/drinks and gather feedback from customers

BPL digital integrated location based marketing using beacons and geolocation technology through our partners Localz and in-depth feedback at the end of each transaction.

The App

Brand new custom design and functionality

iOS and Android

Designed to handle both In-Seat and Click & Collect

Designed for the simplest smoothest flow with ordering and feedback at its core

Image rich design, ease of use and placement of key products

Unique confirmation codes to identify orders

Desktop and Windows, Blackberry platforms alternative

88% of users said it improved their race day experience

Over 90% of users rated it 4 or 5 stars (out of 5)

#2 ranking in the UK App Store for Sports

GN Fast Track users spent £25+ each. An average spend 44% higher than traditional methods.

Customer Feedback

Bespoke customer surveys after each transaction

Customer email addresses to respond to any undesired feedback

Easily edit the questionnaires daily
